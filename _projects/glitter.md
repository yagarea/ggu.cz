Glitter is set of tools for visualising lexical surprise by analysing entropy of outputs of large language models.
This can be used for research of text readability.

Glitter was created as part of PONK project: [ufal.mff.cuni.cz/ponk](https://ufal.mff.cuni.cz/ponk)
